# Copywriting: Voice & Tone Guidelines

When generating text, adhere to this brand persona for Relledomi Entertainment. 

## Brand Personality Keywords
* Bold and culturally rooted (Nairobi x US)
* Immersive and experiential
* Modern, hype-driven, and slightly edgy
* Direct and cinematic

## Grammar & Mechanics rules
* **Headings:** Use Title Case for main headings (H1, H2). Use sentence case for subheadings (H3+). Headings should be punchy and read like event posters.
* **Punctuation:** Be decisive. Use periods for complete sentences. Avoid excessive exclamation points.
* **Clarity:** Prefer active voice and strong verbs over passive voice. Keep sentences tight and rhythmic to match the scroll-driven experience.

## Terminology Guide

| Do Not Use | Use Instead |
| :--- | :--- |
| "Check out our services" | "Business / Work With Us" |
| "Learn more about us" | "The Hub / Agency Info" |
| "Contact us" | "Let's Work" |
| "Interactive website" | "3D Voxel Experience" |
